Game rules

You wake up in a random airport with 2000$. Maximum of 10 of the nearest airports to your location are getting shown, and you get to choose one of them as your next location. Your money decreases according to the distance to the chosen airport (1km costs 2$!). Also, depending on a new airport type and therefore its sustainability rating, you have of chance of keeping, losing, or gaining money. Then the new 10 airports are getting shown, once again. This cycle continues until you run out of money. Each finished game gets saved to your profile, and your best game gets to be in the Global Leaderboard!
